**Thwaite** is an NES game developed by [Damian Yerrick](User_Tepples.xhtml "User:Tepples"), inspired by Atari's _Missile Command_. Point in front of an incoming missile and shoot an [ABM](https://www.britannica.com/technology/antiballistic-missile) at it before the missiles destroy your town.
