# INES Mapper 149

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_149) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_149)

iNES Mapper 149 is just like mapper [145](INES_Mapper_145.xhtml "INES Mapper 145"), but moves the register to overlap with PRG ROM and so has bus conflicts. It's a singleton, PCB labeled SA-0036, and used by the Sachen game _Taiwan Mahjong 16_. 
    
    
     Mask: $8000
     Bus conflicts:
     $8000: [C... ....] - select 8 KiB CHR bank.
