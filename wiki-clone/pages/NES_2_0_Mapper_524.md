# NES 2.0 Mapper 524

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_524) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_524)

NES 2.0 Mapper 524 is used for a bootleg version of Jaleco's _The Lord of King_. Its UNIF board name is **BTL-900218**. 

See [UNIF/900218](UNIF_900218.xhtml "UNIF/900218").
