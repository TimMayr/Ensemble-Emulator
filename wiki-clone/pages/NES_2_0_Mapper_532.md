# NES 2.0 Mapper 532

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_532) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_532)

NES 2.0 Mapper 532 is used for a Chinese translation hack of _Sangokushi II - Haou no Tairiku_ which uses the VirtuaNES EX emulator to overlay Chinese text onto the game's graphics. The UNIF MAPR designation is "CHINA_ER_SAN2"
