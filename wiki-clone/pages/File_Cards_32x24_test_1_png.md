The Latin suits were the easiest to pixel out. I based the red and black jokers seen here on the jack in [the original deck](File_Cards_32x24_png.xhtml "File:Cards 32x24.png") and added a more elaborate cap and contrasting shoulder attachments. But I'm not sure what to do with the knight (N) or the rest of the trumps.
