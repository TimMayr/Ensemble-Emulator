# Licensee codes

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Licensee_codes) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Licensee_codes)

Licensee codes were assigned to companies that released games on Nintendo platforms and were placed in their games' [Nintendo headers](Nintendo_header.xhtml "Nintendo header") or [FDS disk info blocks](FDS_disk_format.xhtml#Disk_info_block_\(block_1\) "FDS disk format"). These 8-bit IDs are mostly consistent across platforms, though there are some differences between those observed here on the NES or FDS and those documented on later platforms like the [Game Boy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gb-archive/salvage/master/txt-files/gbrom.txt). 8-bit codes were later replaced with [2-character codes](http://wiki.cemu.info/wiki/GameIDs#Publisher_Code), which have been in use since.
