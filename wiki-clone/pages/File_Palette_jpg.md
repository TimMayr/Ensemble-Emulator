Palette RAM as physically implemented within the PPU. Note the incomplete address decoding which results in $10/$14/$18/$1C being mirrors of $00/$04/$08/$0C. 

Also of note, palette RAM bits are significantly larger than those of OAM (which are believed to be DRAM).
