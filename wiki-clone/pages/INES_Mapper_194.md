# INES Mapper 194

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_194) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_194)
    
    
     Here are Disch's original notes:  
     ========================
     =  Mapper 194          =
     ========================
     
     
     aka:
     --------------------------
     Pirate MMC3 variant
     
     
     Example Game:
     --------------------------
     Dai-2-Ji - Super Robot Taisen (As)
     
     
     Notes:
     --------------------------
     This mapper is a modified [MMC3](MMC3.xhtml "MMC3") (or is based on MMC3?).
     
     In addition to any CHR-ROM present, there is also an additional 2k of CHR-RAM which is selectable.
     
     CHR Pages $00-$01 are CHR-RAM, other pages are CHR-ROM.
     
     Apart from that, this mapper behaves exactly like your typical MMC3.  See [mapper 004](MMC3.xhtml "INES Mapper 004") for details.
