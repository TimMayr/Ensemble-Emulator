# NES 2.0 Mapper 423

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_423) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_423)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 423** denotes the circuit board used for the _Lexibook Compact Cyber Arcade_ handheld plug'n play consoles. It extends the [VT369](VTxx.xhtml "VTxx")'s 32 MiB address space to 64 MiB through an outer bank register at $4150.
