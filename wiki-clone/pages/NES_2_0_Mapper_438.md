# NES 2.0 Mapper 438

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_438) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_438)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 438** denotes the **K-3071** multicart circuit board for NROM-128/NROM-256 games with selectable mirroring. 
    
    
    A~[1... .... ..PP PPpN] D~[..CC CCCM]
                   || ||||       || |||+- Mirroring (0=Vertical, 1=Horizontal)
                   || ||||       ++-+++-- CHR A17..A13
                   || |||+--------------- PRG mode (0=NROM-128, 1=NROM-256)
                   ++-+++---------------- PRG A18..A14
