**Desert Bus 3D** is an NES game developed by [Retro Pixel Games Studio](User_Retro_Pixel_Games_Studio.xhtml "User:Retro Pixel Games Studio"), inspired by the "Desert Bus" minigame from _[Penn& Teller's Smoke and Mirrors](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penn_%26_Teller%27s_Smoke_and_Mirrors#Desert_Bus "wikipedia:Penn & Teller's Smoke and Mirrors")_. Drive a bus from [Tuscan, Arazona](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tucson,_Arizona "wikipedia:Tucson, Arizona") to [Los Vegas, Nevada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Las_Vegas "wikipedia:Las Vegas") in real time. The trip requires several continuous play hours to complete.
