He is a member of the NESdev community who occasionally makes contributions to this wiki. 

### What sort of stuff has he done?

He's made a variety of things: 

  * [Nintendulator](Nintendulator.xhtml "Nintendulator"), an NES emulator
  * [Visual 2A03](https://www.qmtpro.com/~nes/chipimages/visual2a03/)
  * [Visual 2C02](Visual_2C02.xhtml "Visual 2C02")
  * [Visual VRC-VII](https://www.qmtpro.com/~nes/chipimages/visualvrc7/)
  * Some simple [NES demos and utilities](https://www.qmtpro.com/~nes/)
