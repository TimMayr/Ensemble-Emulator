# User:Tepples/Untested controllers

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/User%3ATepples/Untested_controllers) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#User_Tepples_Untested_controllers)

[Damian Yerrick](User_Tepples.xhtml "User:Tepples") has made demos for the [standard controller](Standard_controller.xhtml "Standard controller") through [Four Score](Four_player_adapters.xhtml "Four Score"), the [Zapper](Zapper.xhtml "Zapper"), the Super NES [Mouse](Mouse.xhtml "Mouse"), the [Arkanoid controller](Arkanoid_controller.xhtml "Arkanoid controller"), and the [Power Pad](Power_Pad.xhtml "Power Pad"). He's interested in making demos using the following [controllers](Input_devices.xhtml "Input devices") but lacks necessary hardware to test them. Owning this hardware would allow documenting additional details of their operation here on the wiki, such as the ~8 ms ADC delay of an Arkanoid controller.
