A background planning sheet inspired by the ones Nintendo's artists used while working on screen layouts for _The Legend of Zelda_[[1]](http://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?p=55733#p55733) and _Super Mario Bros._[[2]](http://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?t=5991)

Includes title block, nametable, attribute table, palette, and pattern table. Sized to fit on one US Letter or A4 page at 150 dpi. Grid units are 24x21 pixels. This isn't square on purpose; instead, it matches [the 8:7 pixel aspect ratio of the NTSC NES](Overscan.xhtml "Overscan").
