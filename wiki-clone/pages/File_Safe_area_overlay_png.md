## Summary

Transparent overlay showing three [safe areas](Overscan.xhtml "Overscan") inset from the screen border: 

  * "Action" (yellow, top and bottom 8 pixels)
  * "Majority" (blue, top 16 pixels, bottom 12 pixels, left and right 8 pixels)
  * "Title" (gray, top and bottom 24 pixels, left and right 8 pixels)
