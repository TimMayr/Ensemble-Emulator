This is a modified version of [this Famicom wiring diagram](https://www.nesdev.org/Ntd_8bit.jpg), altered to depict the NES (unsure if a frontloader or toploader). 

Writing at top-left says: 
    
    
    CPU基板回路図
    

Possible translation: 
    
    
    CPU board schematic
    

Writing at top-right appears (taking some license, given the low scan resolution) to say: 
    
    
    注)回路図の破線部はX'tal(水晶振動子)が賛13⁄64又は青のチューブ付の場合です。
    なお、灰色のX'talの場合は下記の回路となります。
    

Possible translation: 
    
    
    Note)The broken-line section of the schematic is for the situation with X'tal(crystal oscillator) marked 13/64 or enclosed in blue tubing.
    Yet, for a gray X'tal situation it becomes the circuit below.
    

Writing at bottom is clearly truncated, but appears to concern the use of 0.01uF capacitors ("condenser").
