# NES 2.0 Mapper 410

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_410) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_410)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 410** is a variant of [mapper 45](INES_Mapper_045.xhtml "INES Mapper 045") where the ASIC's PRG A21/CHR A20 output (set by bit 6 of the [third write to $6000](INES_Mapper_045.xhtml#CHR-AND.2C_CHR-OR.2FPRG-OR_MSB_.28.246000_.232.2C_write.29 "INES Mapper 045")) selects between regularly-banked CHR-ROM (=0) and 8 KiB of unbanked CHR-RAM (=1). It is used solely for the _Super 8-in-1 - 98格鬥天王＋熱血_ (JY-302) multicart.
