# INES Mapper 039

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_039) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_039)

Nestopia's and MESS's source claims that iNES Mapper 039 is assigned to Subor's “Study & Game 32-in-1”. But what they implement looks identical to oversize [BNROM](INES_Mapper_034.xhtml "BNROM") fully supported with [iNES Mapper 241](INES_Mapper_241.xhtml "INES Mapper 241").
