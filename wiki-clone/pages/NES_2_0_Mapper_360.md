# NES 2.0 Mapper 360

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_360) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_360)

NES 2.0 mapper is used for Bit Corp's 31-in-1 multicart (3150). Instead of presenting a menu, five user-accessible DIP switches directly select the outer 16 KiB bank. Only if all DIP switches are zero does the outer bank size become 32 KiB (for _Super Mario Brothers_). The fifth DIP switch, which selects highest outer bank bit 0x10s, also selects vertical (0) or horizontal (1) nametable mirroring.
