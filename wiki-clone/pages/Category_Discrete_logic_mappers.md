# Category:Discrete logic mappers

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Category%3ADiscrete_logic_mappers) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Category_Discrete_logic_mappers)

Discrete logic [mappers](Mapper.xhtml "MMC") are made almost entirely using stock components, especially the commonly available [74-series chips](Category_7400_series.xhtml "Category:7400 series"). More often than not, discrete mappers are susceptible to [bus conflicts](Category_Mappers_with_bus_conflicts.xhtml "Category:Mappers with bus conflicts"). 

See [User:Lidnariq/Discrete Logic Table](User_Lidnariq_Discrete_Logic_Table.xhtml "User:Lidnariq/Discrete Logic Table"), [User:Lidnariq/MMC3 Variants#Discrete logic mappers](User_Lidnariq_MMC3_Variants.xhtml#Discrete_logic_mappers "User:Lidnariq/MMC3 Variants") and [Comparison of Nintendo mappers#Discrete_logic](Comparison_of_Nintendo_mappers.xhtml#Discrete_logic "Comparison of Nintendo mappers") for alternative ways to compare these mappers.
