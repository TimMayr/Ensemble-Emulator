[Ophis](http://michaelcmartin.github.io/Ophis/) | Michael C. Martin and contributors  | (anywhere with Python support)  | A cross-assembler for the 6502 series of microprocessors.  | The apparent successor to P65.   
[P65](https://hkn.eecs.berkeley.edu/~mcmartin/P65/) | Michael C. Martin  | (anywhere with Perl support)  | An old Perl-based set of 6502 assemblers.  | Dropped in favor of Ophis (see above).   
[Telemark Cross Assembler](https://web.archive.org/web/20151021120410/http://home.comcast.net/~tasm/) | Squak Valley Software  | DOS  | A shareware assembler for numerous 8-bit processors, including the 6502, Z80, and 8051.  | Website seems to have disappeared as of 2016.

[WLA DX](https://github.com/vhelin/wla-dx) | Ville Helin and contributors  | Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, etc. (Source code available) | "Yet Another GB-Z80/Z80/6502/65C02/6510/65816/HUC6280/SPC-700 Multi Platform Cross Assembler Package" |

[XTOOLS](http://www.bipom.com/products/us/676.html) | BiPOM Electronics  | Windows?  | Commercial set of assemblers, disassemblers, and monitors for various platforms (including 6502).  |
