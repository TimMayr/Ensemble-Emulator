# $4017

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/%244017) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#_4017)

$4017 is an [APU](APU.xhtml "APU") or [Joypad](Controller_reading.xhtml "Controller reading") register. 

  * When writing to this address, it affects the [APU Frame Counter](APU.xhtml#Frame_Counter_\(%244017\) "APU").
  * When reading from this address, it [ reads Player 2's controller.](Controller_reading.xhtml#%244016_/_%244017_Read "Controller reading")
