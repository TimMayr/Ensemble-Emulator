# INES Mapper 224

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_224) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_224)

iNES Mapper 224 is used for the 晶科泰 (Jncota) KT-008 PCB. It includes the same chipset as the "MINDKIDS" multicarts ([NES 2.0 Mapper 268](NES_2_0_Mapper_268.xhtml "NES 2.0 Mapper 268"), Submapper 1).
