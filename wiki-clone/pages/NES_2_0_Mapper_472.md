# NES 2.0 Mapper 472

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_472) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_472)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 472** denotes the 恒格 **FK-206 JG** [MMC3](MMC3.xhtml "MMC3")-compatible PCB. It is basically mapper [52](INES_Mapper_052.xhtml "INES Mapper 052") with the bits reshuffled.
