# Category:Pinouts

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Category%3APinouts) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Category_Pinouts)

Various [hardware pinouts](Hardware_pinout.xhtml "Hardware pinout"). 

Generic pinout legend: 
    
    
               .---\/---.
      input -> | 01  08 | <- input
     output <- | 02  07 | -> output
     supply -- | 03  06 | ?? unknown functionality: could be an input, no connection, or a supply line
       misc -- | 04  05 | <> bidirectional
               '--------'
    

A signal labeled "NC" means "Not Connected."
