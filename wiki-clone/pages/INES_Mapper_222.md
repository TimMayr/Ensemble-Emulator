# INES Mapper 222

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_222) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_222)

iNES Mapper 222 reportedly represents pirate ports of [Namco's Dragon Ninja](http://bootgod.dyndns.org:7777/profile.php?id=2303) (on the PCB **CTC-31**) and [Taito's Don Doko Don 2](http://bootgod.dyndns.org:7777/profile.php?id=4519) (renamed "Super Mario Bros. 8"). 

It appears to use a [VRC2](VRC2_and_VRC4.xhtml "VRC2") and a bunch of 7400-series logic to form some kind of interrupt controller. 

No better information than FCEUX's source is currently available. The [cartridge shell](http://cah4e3.shedevr.org.ru/collection/_Dragon_Ninja.jpg) doesn't even hint which corporation CTC is.
