# NES 2.0 Mapper 426

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_426) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_426)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 426** denotes VT369-based handheld plug'n play consoles from that connect a 256-byte serial ROM to the VT369's GPIO port at $4150. The serial ROM's data is included as NES 2.0 Misc. ROM at the end of the ROM image file.
