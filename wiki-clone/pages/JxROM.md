# JxROM

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/JxROM) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#JxROM)

The generic designation **JxROM** refers to cartridge boards made by Nintendo of Europe that use the [Sunsoft FME-7](Sunsoft_FME_7.xhtml "Sunsoft FME-7") mapper IC. These boards are the European counterpart to boards manufactured by Sunsoft that used the FME-7, including several Famicom boards and the North American **NES-BTR**. The [iNES](INES.xhtml "INES") format assigns [Mapper 69](Sunsoft_FME_7.xhtml "INES Mapper 069") to JxROM. 

Nintendo manufactured these JxROM boards for the European market:

---|---|---|---|---  
JLROM | 256 KiB |  | 128 KiB ROM   
JSROM | 128 KiB | 8 KiB (no battery) | 256 KiB ROM | equivalent to BTR
