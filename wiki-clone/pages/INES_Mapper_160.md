# INES Mapper 160

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_160) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_160)

**iNES Mapper 160** appeared to describe variant behavior of [iNES Mapper 090](J_Y__Company_ASIC.xhtml "INES Mapper 090"), but Mapper 90 fully encompasses the behavior Mapper 160 was intended to emulate.
