# Category:FDS conversion mappers

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Category%3AFDS_conversion_mappers) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Category_FDS_conversion_mappers)

These mappers describe boards used in unlicensed cartridge conversions of Famicom Disk System games. Designed to accomodate individual games without having to relocate their program code and thus having to modify all absolute offsets, they often involve unusual banking schemes. Most of these cartridges were made by [Kaiser](http://bootleggames.wikia.com/wiki/Kaiser) (KS-xxxx) and Whirlwind Manu.
