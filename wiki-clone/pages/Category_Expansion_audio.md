# Category:Expansion audio

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Category%3AExpansion_audio) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Category_Expansion_audio)

The [Famicom](Family_Computer.xhtml "Famicom") had the capability of audio expansion, via an audio return loop on its [cartridge connector](Cartridge_connector.xhtml "Cartridge connector"). Cartridges containing additional hardware would mix the [incoming 2A03 audio](APU.xhtml "APU") signal with their own audio signal produced by the [mapper](Mapper.xhtml "Mapper") chip. The [FDS](Family_Computer_Disk_System.xhtml "FDS") also produced expansion sound which operated through the same cartridge connector.
