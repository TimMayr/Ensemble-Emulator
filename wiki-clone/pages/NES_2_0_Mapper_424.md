# NES 2.0 Mapper 424

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_424) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_424)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 424** denotes the circuit board used for the _Lexibook Retro TV Game Console_ plug'n play consoles. It extends the [VT369](VTxx.xhtml "VTxx")'s 32 MiB address space to 64 MiB through an outer bank register at $411E.
