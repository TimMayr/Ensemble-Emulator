# NES 2.0 Mapper 469

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_469) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_469)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 469** denotes BlazePro multicarts consisting of games originally released for the Famicom Disk System. The circuit board is physically identical to [NES 2.0 Mapper 468](NES_2_0_Mapper_468.xhtml "NES 2.0 Mapper 468"), but the CPLD is programmed differently.
