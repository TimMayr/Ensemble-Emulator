# NES 2.0 Mapper 517

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_517) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_517)

NES 2.0 Mapper 517 is used for 까치와 노래친구 (Kkachi-wa Nolae Chingu), a Korean Karaoke game. It uses banking similar to [UNROM](UxROM.xhtml "UNROM") without bus conflicts, but has additional ADC hardware (different from [NES 2.0 Mapper 515](NES_2_0_Mapper_515.xhtml "NES 2.0 Mapper 515")) for its custom microphone socket at $6000 and $6001.
