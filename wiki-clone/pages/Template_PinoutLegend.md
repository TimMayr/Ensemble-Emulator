**Pinout legend**
    
    
        -- |  power supply   | --
        <- |     output      | ->
        -> |      input      | <-
        <> |  bidirectional  | <>
        ?? |   unknown use   | ??
    
    n or f - connects to NES or Famicom
     r - connects to ROMs (PRG ROM, CHR ROM, CHR RAM)
     w - connects to WRAM (PRG RAM)
    ?? - could be an input, no connection, or a supply line
