# INES Mapper 216

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_216) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_216)

iNES Mapper 216 is used for certain games in Russian, including _Bonza_ and _Videopoker Bonza_ as well as _Magic Jewelry II_ by RCM Group. 

Games using this mapper, or certain revisions thereof, may require Dendy timings to display correctly.
