2C04 palette (yes, all four PPUs), Voronoi tessellation. 

Angle = hue, radius = 1-luminance. 

All but two colors (and the six greys) on the 2C04 are 100% saturated in HSL space. (Those two colors are a light grey-orange and a light grey-purple)
