# NES 2.0 Mapper 408

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_408) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_408)

NES 2.0 Mapper 408 is used for Konami Collector's Series Advance Arcade Plug & Play. It works similarly to Mapper 256 but switches between CHR-ROM and CHR-RAM by repurposing higher address lines.
