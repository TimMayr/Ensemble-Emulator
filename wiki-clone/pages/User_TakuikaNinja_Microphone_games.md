# User:TakuikaNinja/Microphone games

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/User%3ATakuikaNinja/Microphone_games) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#User_TakuikaNinja_Microphone_games)

The following is a list of games which utilise the microphone found on the [ Famicom's](Family_Computer.xhtml "Famicom") hard-wired second [ controller](Standard_controller.xhtml "Standard controller"), along with their in-game functionality. The microphone was removed from the New/AV Famicom and NES systems, the latter of which lead to some western localisations having to remove or replace the microphone functionality. Such alterations are also listed when applicable. Emulators may offer an input mapping to the microphone in order to fully support these games.

_Bungeling Bay (Raid on Bungeling Bay)_ | Lets player 2 summon jets in the two player mode. | Unaltered, so it is inaccessible by normal means.
