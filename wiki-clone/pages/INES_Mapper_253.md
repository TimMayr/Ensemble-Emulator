# INES Mapper 253

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/INES_Mapper_253) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#INES_Mapper_253)

iNES Mapper 253 is used for Waixing's Chinese localization of the Japanese Bandai game _Dragon Ball Z: 強襲! サイヤ人 (Dragon Ball Z: Kyōshū! Saiya-jin)_. It uses a clone of the Konami [VRC4](VRC2_and_VRC4.xhtml "VRC4"), with the chip's A0 connected to CPU A2 and the chip's A1 connected to CPU A3 (VRC4e, Mapper 23 submapper 2). It has 8 KiB of PRG-RAM at CPU $6000-$7FFF, and 1 KiB CHR pages 4 and 5 are CHR-RAM. 

[INES Mapper 252](INES_Mapper_252.xhtml "INES Mapper 252") is the same, except that it's 1 KiB CHR pages 6 and 7 that are CHR-RAM. 

Mappers [74](INES_Mapper_074.xhtml "INES Mapper 074"), [192](INES_Mapper_192.xhtml "INES Mapper 192"), [194](INES_Mapper_194.xhtml "INES Mapper 194"), and [195](INES_Mapper_195.xhtml "INES Mapper 195"), also used for Waixing's localizations, are similar but use an MMC3.
