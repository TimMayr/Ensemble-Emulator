# CxROM

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/CxROM) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#CxROM)

The designation **CxROM** refers to 2 distinct Nintendo cartridge boards - NES-[CNROM](CNROM.xhtml "CNROM") and NES-[CPROM](CPROM.xhtml "CPROM") \- as well as their [HVC](Family_Computer.xhtml "Famicom") counterparts, and several third-party variants.
