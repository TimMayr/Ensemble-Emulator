# NES 2.0 Mapper 530

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_530) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_530)

NES 2.0 Mapper 530 is used for _Super Mario Bros. Pocker Mali_ (sic), a bootleg version of Bandai's _Crayon Shin-chan: Ora to Poi Poi_. It's a [VRC4](VRC2_and_VRC4.xhtml "VRC4") clone (A0/A1, VRC4f) with the PRG and CHR address lines scrambled: PRG A14 is swapped with PRG A16, and CHR A15 is swapped with CHR A16. Its UNIF board name is **UNL-AX5705**.
