# NES 2.0 Mapper 557

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_557) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_557)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 557** denotes the NTDEC **2718** circuit board, used for the LG25 cartridge conversion of the Famicom Disk System game _Moero TwinBee: Cinnamon-hakase o Sukue!_.
