# Category:Multi-ASIC mappers

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Category%3AMulti-ASIC_mappers) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Category_Multi_ASIC_mappers)

Some mappers can mimic the functionality of several other ASIC-based mappers, such as [MMC1](MMC1.xhtml "MMC1") and [MMC3](MMC3.xhtml "MMC3"). Some just implemented several ASICs on the same die, while others are based on CPLD or FPGA components.
