**Welcome to NESdev Wiki.**

Certain edit summary patterns have been used in the past by automated processes to [deface wikis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Vandalism "wikipedia:Wikipedia:Vandalism"). This wiki restricts edit summaries that resemble these. If you plan to make constructive contributions, try using more than one word in your edit summary.
