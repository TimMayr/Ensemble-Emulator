# Rare

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Rare) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Rare)

**Rare Ltd.** is a video game studio founded by Tim and Chris Stamper. Many of their games used [AxROM](AxROM.xhtml "AxROM") boards. Several games by Rare, such as _Battletoads_ , are harsh [tests for emulators](Tricky_to_emulate_games.xhtml "Tricky-to-emulate games") because they depend on so many NES timing quirks.

## After the NES era

From 1994 to 2003, Rare developed games like _GoldenEye 007_ , _Banjo-Kazooie_ , and _Perfect Dark_ exclusively for Nintendo platforms. In 2003, Microsoft bought Rare.
