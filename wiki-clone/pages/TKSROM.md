# TKSROM

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/TKSROM) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#TKSROM)

**TKSROM** refers to a variant of the [TKROM](TxROM.xhtml "TKROM") board with the VRAM mirroring signals routed through the CHR side of the [Nintendo MMC3](MMC3.xhtml "MMC3") a different way, as described in [iNES Mapper 118](INES_Mapper_118.xhtml "INES Mapper 118"). It is used only for the Japanese game _Ys III: Wanderers from Ys_. 

## Overview

  * PRG ROM size: 128, 256 or 512 KB (DIP-28/32 Nintendo pinout)
  * PRG ROM bank size: 8 KB
  * PRG RAM: 8 KB with optional battery
  * CHR capacity: 128 KB ROM (DIP-32 Nintendo pinout)
  * CHR bank size: 1 KB and 2 KB
  * Nametable [mirroring](Mirroring.xhtml "Mirroring"): Controlled by mapper, but in a nonstandard way (see Mapper 118).
  * Subject to [bus conflicts](Bus_conflict.xhtml "Bus conflict"): No
