# NES 2.0 Mapper 259

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_259) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_259)

NES 2.0 Mapper 259 has been tentatively reserved by the FCEUX developers to mean the same hardware as UNIF MAPR `F-15`, an MMC3 multicart.
