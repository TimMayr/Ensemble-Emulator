# SHARP IX0043AE pinout

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/SHARP_IX0043AE_pinout) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#SHARP_IX0043AE_pinout)

SHARP IX0043AE: 16-pin DIP 0.3 
    
    
             .---v---.
    EYE2+ <- |01   16| -- +5V
    EYE2- <- |02   15| <- /RESET
             |03   14| -> EYE1-
             |04   13| -> EYE1+
             |05   12|
    XTAL1 -- |06   11|
    XTAL2 -- |07   10|
      GND -- |08   09| -> V+-GEN
             '-------'
