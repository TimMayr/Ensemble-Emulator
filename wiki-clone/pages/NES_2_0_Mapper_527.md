# NES 2.0 Mapper 527

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_527) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_527)

NES 2.0 Mapper 527 is used for a bootleg version of Taito's 不動明王伝 (Fudō Myōō Den). Its UNIF board name is **UNL-AX-40G**. The original [INES Mapper 207](INES_Mapper_207.xhtml "INES Mapper 207") is replaced with a [VRC2](VRC2_and_VRC4.xhtml "VRC2") clone (A0/A1, i.e. VRC2b) while retaining Mapper 207's extended mirroring. Accordingly, CHR A17 is connected to CIRAM A10.
