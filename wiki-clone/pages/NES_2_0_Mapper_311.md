# NES 2.0 Mapper 311

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_311) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_311)

NES 2.0 Mapper 311 was assigned in error for what was believed to be a variant of the Super Mario Bros. 2 pirate cart emulated as Mapper 43, but turned out to just be a bad dump of that cartridge.
