# Mouse

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/Mouse) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#Mouse)

Three controllers analogous to a computer **mouse** are known to be compatible with software for the NES or FC, either directly or through a simple adapter. 

  * [Super NES Mouse](Super_NES_Mouse.xhtml "Super NES Mouse")
  * [Hori Track](Hori_Track.xhtml "Hori Track")
  * [Subor Mouse](Subor_Mouse.xhtml "Subor Mouse")
