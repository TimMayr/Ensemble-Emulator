# NES 2.0 Mapper 470

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_470) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_470)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 470** denotes the **INX_007T_V01** multicart circuit board, used for the Retro-Bit re-release of _Battletoads and Double Dragon_. It is basically [AOROM](AxROM.xhtml "AOROM") with an additional outer bank register at $5000-$5FFF whose data selects the 256 KiB outer bank. The outer bank register is cleared to $00 on reset.
