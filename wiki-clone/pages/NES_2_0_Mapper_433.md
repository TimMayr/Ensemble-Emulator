# NES 2.0 Mapper 433

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_433) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_433)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 433** denotes the **NC-20MB** PCB, used for the _20-in-1 (CA-006)_ multicart. It is almost identical to [INES Mapper 233](INES_Mapper_233.xhtml "INES Mapper 233"), except that mirroring is selected just by single bit 6 (1=Horizontal).
