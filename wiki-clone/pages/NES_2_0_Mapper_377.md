# NES 2.0 Mapper 377

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_377) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_377)

NES 2.0 Mapper 377 is used for the _1998 Super Game 8-in-1 (JY-111)_ pirate multicart. It works similarly to Mapper 267 except it has an outer 256KiB PRG-ROM bank.
