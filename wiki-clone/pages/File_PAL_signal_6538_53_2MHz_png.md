One field of raw composite video from a PAL famiclone resampled to 53.2 MHz (twice the PAL master clock), for testing [PAL video](PAL_video.xhtml "PAL video") decoders. Ten pixels in this capture correspond to one NES pixel; twelve correspond to one cycle of the color subcarrier, allowing use of a PAL-adapted version of something like [Bisqwit's QAM chroma decoder](http://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?p=172329#p172329). 

Based on the [250 MHz signal](File_PAL_signal_6538_250MHz_png.xhtml "File:PAL signal 6538 250MHz.png") that [HardWareMan captured](http://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?p=173791#p173791).
