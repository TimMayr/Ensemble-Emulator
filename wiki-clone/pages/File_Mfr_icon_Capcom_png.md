Capcom used Nintendo-manufactured boards, but one [mapper](Mapper.xhtml "Mapper") was used only for a single Capcom game. A yellow "C" in [ITC Korinna Extra Bold](https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/korinna/) with a blue outline represents Capcom. 

Incidentally, clue screens on the TV show _Jeopardy!_ (but not any NES adaptation of _Jeopardy!_) [also uses Korinna](https://twitter.com/rusty_shackles/status/686295371941810177), as do scene titles in _Frasier_.
