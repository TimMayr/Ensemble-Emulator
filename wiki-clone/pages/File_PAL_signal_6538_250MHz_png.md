[![Media as described below](../wiki-images/PAL_signal_6538_250MHz.png)](../wiki-images/PAL_signal_6538_250MHz.png)

Raw 250 MHz capture of one field of composite output from the UMC UA6538 PPU in a PAL famiclone running a palette demo. The dark stripe at far left is the horizontal sync pulse. The authentic 2C07 produces a similar signal. Use this to test your emulator's [PAL video](PAL_video.xhtml "PAL video") decoder, or to investigate causes of jailbars.
