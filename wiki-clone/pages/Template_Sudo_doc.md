If you have an idea for a fairly uncontroversial change to a given page that you lack privileges to edit, describe the edit on the corresponding talk page and add `{{[sudo](Template_Sudo.xhtml "Template:Sudo")}}` to get an administrator's attention. For example, you could propose a change to the [Main Page](Main_Page.xhtml "Main Page") by adding a section to [Talk:Main Page](https://www.nesdev.org/w/index.php?title=Talk:Main_Page&action=edit&redlink=1 "Talk:Main Page \(page does not exist\)") and putting `{{[sudo](Template_Sudo.xhtml "Template:Sudo")}}` at the top of your comment.
