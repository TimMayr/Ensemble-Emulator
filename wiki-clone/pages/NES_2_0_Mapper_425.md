# NES 2.0 Mapper 425

From [NESdev Wiki](Nesdev_Wiki.xhtml) | View [current version](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/NES_2.0_Mapper_425) | View [other pages](Special_AllPages.xhtml#NES_2_0_Mapper_425)

**NES 2.0 Mapper 425** denotes the circuit board used for [VT369](VTxx.xhtml "VTxx")-based handheld plug'n play consoles from Cube Tech. It extends the [VT369](VTxx.xhtml "VTxx")'s 32 MiB address space to 128 MiB through an outer bank register at $41E6.
